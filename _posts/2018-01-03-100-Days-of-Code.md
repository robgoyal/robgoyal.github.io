Alexander Kallaway is the developer behind the 100 Days of Code movement. The 100 Days of Code has two basic steps associated with it. Step 1 is to code for 1 hour every day for 100 days. Step 2 is to publicly commit to coding for 1 hour every day. This blog will maintain as that public commitment.

<!--more-->

You can read more about the rules and details about the challenge [here](https://medium.freecodecamp.org/join-the-100daysofcode-556ddb4579e4).

The rules mention to publicly tweet your progress but since I don't have a twitter, I thought it'd be better to make use of my blog and create a blog post for each day about my progress. This will allow me to explain in more detail what I have done. It's also useful to log what I have achieved each day.

The format of the blog will be to discuss the project(s) that I am currently working on, any challenges that I faced while working on the project(s) and what I learned while coding.

This is the first time I am undertaking this challenge and it will be the first round. For Round 2, I hope to work on more complex problems and projects. For Round 1, the projects are going to be a bit more simplified but complex for a beginner like myself nonetheless.

Tomorrow will be Day 1 of this challenge.
